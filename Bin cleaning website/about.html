About our Company

The Trash Can Cleaners began in San Antonio, Texas as a small family project. As parents dealing with messy, smelly bins, we realized there had to be a better, more sanitary way to keep them clean.

With combined experience in industrial pressure washing, mechanics, fabrication, and cleaning chemicals, we built our own system from the ground up — launching the first trash bin cleaning service in San Antonio and one of the earliest in Texas.

What started as a simple solution for our family quickly grew into something bigger. Today, we help people across North America launch and grow successful bin cleaning businesses with equipment built by a team that has done it themselves.
